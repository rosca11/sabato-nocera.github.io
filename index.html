Research Interests

  • Empirical Software Engineering
  • Software Security
  • Software Supply Chain
My research interests are mainly in Empirical Software Engineering and involve Software Security and Software Supply Chain. In this respect, my Ph.D. research project focuses on Software Bills of Materials (SBOMs), machine-readable records containing the details and supply chain relationships of the open-source and proprietary components used in building software artifacts. SBOMs enable software supply chain visibility, facilitating the identification of components with known vulnerabilities and licensing issues. My research aims to investigate the need for SBOMs to support the software supply chain and provide tools to produce and consume better SBOMs.
